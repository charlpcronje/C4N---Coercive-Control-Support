100% Local, 100% Private

Your Data Never Leaves Your Device

Everything you mark, every note you write, and all your interactions stay completely private on your device. We cannot see your data, access your data, or share your data‚Äîbecause we never receive it.

How Your Data is Stored

C4N uses modern web technologies to ensure your information remains completely private and secure on your own device.

IndexedDB (Local Database)

  • What it is: A browser-based database that stores data directly on your device
  • Where it's stored: In your browser's secure storage area on your computer or phone
  • Who can access it: Only you, through this application
  • Capacity: Can store megabytes of data without performance issues
Technical Detail: IndexedDB is a W3C standard supported by all modern browsers. It provides structured, indexed storage that's faster and more capable than cookies or localStorage.

What Gets Stored Locally

  • Your checkbox selections (items you've marked as experienced)
  • Personal notes you've added to behavior items
  • The behavior patterns database (downloaded once, cached for speed)
  • Your preferences and settings

Zero Server Communication

Your personal interactions with this application do not communicate with any server. Here's exactly what does and doesn't get sent:

‚úÖ What Gets Sent to Servers

  • Initial page load: The application files (HTML, JavaScript, CSS)
  • Database update check: A tiny request to check if content has been updated (no personal data)
  • Text-to-speech (optional): Only when you click "Listen," the text is sent to generate audio

‚ùå What NEVER Gets Sent

  • Your checkbox selections
  • Your personal notes
  • Which pages you visit
  • How long you spend on the app
  • Any personally identifying information

Technical Security Measures

HTTPS Encryption

All communication between your browser and our server uses HTTPS encryption, ensuring that even the minimal data transferred (like loading the application) is secure.

No Tracking or Analytics

Unlike most websites, C4N does not use:

  • Google Analytics or any analytics platform
  • Tracking cookies
  • Social media pixels
  • Third-party scripts (except the text-to-speech API, only when you request it)

No User Accounts

There are no accounts, logins, passwords, or user profiles. This means:

  • No email addresses collected
  • No password to forget or be compromised
  • No profile information stored anywhere
  • Complete anonymity

Clearing Your Data

Since all data is stored locally in your browser, you have complete control:

Export Your Data First (Optional)

Use the "Export Data" button in the sidebar to save a JSON file of your selections and notes before clearing.

Clear Browser Data

  • Chrome/Edge: Settings ‚Üí Privacy and Security ‚Üí Clear browsing data ‚Üí Check "Cookies and site data"
  • Firefox: Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Clear Data ‚Üí Check "Cookies and Site Data"
  • Safari: Settings ‚Üí Safari ‚Üí Clear History and Website Data
Important: Clearing browser data will remove ALL data from C4N on that device. This cannot be undone unless you've exported your data first.

Offline Capability

Once loaded, C4N works completely offline. Your browser caches the application files, meaning:

  • You can use it without an internet connection
  • Your data never risks being transmitted even if connected
  • Faster performance since everything loads from your device
